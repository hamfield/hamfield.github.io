About

The Crawlhatch is a one-man Alternative Rock/Metal band consisting of me, myself, the person who is me, and I...

The name is created out of the way I felt at that moment- Stuck in a hatch, underground, crawling back out to the surface.

Hencefore "The Crawlhatch" is the name for my one man band project.

Made out of boredom, directed by hyperfocus.

I've composed a few experimental songs in 2025.

But then circumstances changed so I have decided to pause this project for now.

I might revive this project in around mid-2027 or later.
